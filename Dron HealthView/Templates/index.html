Welcome to HealthView

HealthView is a personal health tracking app designed to help individuals monitor their health and wellness. With HealthView, you can track important health metrics like heart rate, blood pressure, and sleep patterns using wearable devices. Our app also stores your medical history and sends reminders for medications, making it easy to stay on top of your health.

Key features

Your Dashboard

HealthView provides a personalized health dashboard that allows individuals to track their health metrics such as heart rate, blood pressure, and sleep patterns through wearables and it also stores the medical history of the individual. HealthView also sends reminders to individuals to take their medications on time, ensuring that they adhere to their prescribed medication regimen.

Medical Providers

The nearby medical provider feature in HealthView allows users to easily search for medical providers in their area, view their contact information, and get directions to their offices. This feature is particularly useful for individuals who are new to an area or in need of urgent medical care. It allows users to give ratings to the providers.

First Aid Information

The first aid information feature of HealthView provides users with access to important medical information and instructions on how to handle emergency situations. This includes step-by-step guidance on how to perform basic first aid procedures such as CPR and treating burns. By providing users with this information, HealthView empowers them to take control of their health and be better prepared for emergencies.

Medical History Access

In an emergency, HealthView can provide the patient's medical history to the medical attendant, which can help the medical attendant give optimal treatment. This can be crucial in situations where the patient is unable to communicate their medical history.
