First Aid Tips

Be prepared for any emergency

Knowing the right first aid techniques can help save a life in an emergency situation. Here are some important first aid tips to keep in mind.

Chockingr

This occurs when an object gets stuck in a person's throat, blocking their airway. Quick action is required to remove the object and restore breathing.

Burns

Burns can be caused by heat, electricity, chemicals, or radiation. The severity of a burn can range from mild to life-threatening, and first aid will depend on the type and severity of the burn.

Fractures

A fracture is a break in a bone. It's important to immobilize the affected area and seek medical attention.
